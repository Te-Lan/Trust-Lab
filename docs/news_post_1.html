Director Huang Visited The School of Journalism and Communication at Peking University

September 24, 2024

On April 15, Professor Huang Yihui, Chair and Chair Professor of the Department of Media and Communication at City University of Hong Kong, visited the School of Journalism and Communication at Peking University for an exchange. The meeting was attended by Dean Chen Gang, Party Secretary Tang Jinnan, Vice Deans Chen Kaihe and Wang Weijia, as well as Xu Jing, the program leader of the Master's in Health Communication. Both parties held an in-depth discussion on cooperation in areas such as academic development, talent cultivation, and exchange visits.

Dean Chen Gang warmly welcomed the visit of Professor Huang Yihui, Chair of the Department. Following the welcome, both parties introduced the history, research focus, faculty strength, and student development of their respective institutions, expressing hopes for increased future exchanges and collaboration.

During the seminar, both parties engaged in an in-depth discussion about the current international exchange programs for students at the two institutions, exploring potential opportunities for future exchange visits.

After the seminar, Professor Huang Yihui also participated in an interview with students from our department, focusing on topics such as health communication and risk communication. During the interview, Professor Huang encouraged students to maintain their original academic curiosity and passion in the rapidly changing environment, while also emphasizing the importance of building resilience and cultivating inner strength.

Professor Huang Yihui is currently the Chair and Chair Professor of the Department of Media and Communication at City University of Hong Kong, and an Emeritus Professor at the School of Journalism and Communication at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. She holds a PhD from the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland, USA, and has previously served as a professor at both The Chinese University of Hong Kong and National Chengchi University in Taiwan. Her research interests include strategic communication, risk communication in health and technology issues, crisis communication, relationship management, and conflict resolution. She has published over 200 articles in top-tier journals, delivered 26 keynote speeches, and participated in more than 100 academic conferences. Professor Huang serves as the editor of *Communication and the Public* (SCOPUS and ESCI indexed) and *Communication and Society* (TSSCI indexed), and is a member of the editorial boards of several international SSCI journals. In 2021, she was elected as a Fellow of the International Communication Association (ICA), becoming the first Chinese woman from the Greater China region to receive this honor and the eighth Chinese member ever elected since the association's establishment.
