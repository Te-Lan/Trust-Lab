Head and Chair Professor

Yi-Hui Christine Huang Head and Chair Professor

Yi-Hui Christine Huang (Ph.D., University of Maryland) is Head and Chair Professor of the Department of Communication and Media at City University of Hong Kong. She was elected as International Communication Association (ICA) Fellow in 2021 and was conferred as Senior Research Fellow by Hong Kong Research Grants Council (RGC) in 2024. She has been committed to research on strategic communication, risk communication in health and technology, crisis communication, relationship management and conflict resolution, and cross-cultural communication. She currently serves as Editor for Communication and the Public (ESCI) and Communication and Society (TSSCI). She have also been a founding editorial board member for the Journal of International Crisis and Risk Communication. In addition, she serves as an editorial board member for more than 10 major international SSCI journals, such as Journal of Communication, Journal of Public Relations Research, and Public Relations Review.
