Join Us at TRUST Lab!

Are you passionate about exploring the dynamics of trust, risk, and media in times of crisis? The TRUST Lab at the City University of Hong Kong is seeking motivated Research Assistants and Postdoctoral Fellows to join our team.

Be part of groundbreaking research on trust-building, media interventions, and societal resilience in areas like health, technology, and environmental crises. Collaborate with an interdisciplinary team, contribute to impactful publications, and help shape the future of trust research.

Apply for our Research Assistant and Postdoctoral positions to elevate your career and contribute to advancing the understanding and management of trust during critical times.

For inquiries, please contact: comhead@cityu.edu.hk .
